Featured hammock of the day

Why sleep on a bed when you can sleep on a hammock . . .

Some random but definitely useful information.
The word "hammock" comes from the Spanish word "hamaca," which means "thrown or suspended thing."
The oldest known hammock was discovered in the ancient Mayan city of Coba in Mexico, and is believed to be over 1000 years old.
In some parts of the world, hammocks are used as beds. They are especially popular in Central and South America, where they are often used in place of traditional beds.
There are many different types of hammocks, including rope hammocks, quilted hammocks, and fabric hammocks. Some are designed for use in the outdoors, while others are made for indoor use.
